# Super Agency Roadmap 2026

## Executive Summary

**Super Agency** is an autonomous intelligence company-of-companies framework that enables ethical, local-first AI systems to design, ship, and operate digital and physical businesses with human-aligned governance. The system operates on Resonance Energy principles: local-first, consent & control, provenance-first, unit economics, resilience, ethical rails, council governance, and "do less, go deeper."

**Current Status**: MVP operational with core agent orchestration, NCL Second Brain, and YouTube content ingestion pipeline.
