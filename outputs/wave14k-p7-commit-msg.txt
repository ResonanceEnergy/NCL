Wave 14K Phase 7: realistic frictions + iOS dashboard surface (K6a/b/c)

Closes the realism + visibility loop. Paper trades now reflect the
slippage and partial-fill risk a live order would face; the friction
model self-calibrates from observed close data; the iOS Auto-Trader
card has a single rollup endpoint to consume.

NEW: runtime/portfolio/auto_trader/friction_profile.py (~300 LOC)

K6a — Per-strategy FrictionProfile {slippage_bps, partial_fill_prob,
partial_fill_min_pct, asset_type}. Per-asset-type defaults:
  stock:   3 bps, 0% partial
  options: 50 bps, 5% partial (min 30% qty)
  crypto:  10 bps, 0% partial
  futures: 15 bps, 0% partial

apply_friction_to_payload() injects realistic friction at open time:
  - LONG entries fill HIGHER than the limit by slippage_bps (paid more)
  - SHORT entries fill LOWER than the limit (got less)
  - Probability-gated partial fills reduce quantity to a sampled
    fraction in [partial_fill_min_pct, 1.0]
  - Original entry_price + quantity preserved in scanner_data.friction
    for K6b calibration to read back

Wired into loop._idea_to_paper_payload — every auto-trader open now
gets per-strategy friction applied before hitting the paper engine.
Non-blocking (open succeeds at unfricted price if friction lookup
fails).

K6b — calibrate_from_closes(strategy, window=50): reads recent closed
paper trades, extracts the applied_bps from scanner_data.friction
metadata, EMA-blends into the profile's slippage_bps with learning_
rate (default 0.1). Per-strategy n_observed counter + last 50 bps
samples preserved for audit.

maybe_calibrate(strategy, n_closed) auto-fires every
NCL_FRICTION_CALIB_EVERY_N (default 10) closes per strategy, called
from outcome_attributor.attribute_close after the bandit/SHAP/drift
chain. Persists to friction_profiles.json + appends each calibration
to friction_calibrations.jsonl.

K6c — New /auto-trader/dashboard rollup endpoint for iOS consumption:
ONE call returns operator state, top-5 strategies by Bayesian LCB,
drift status counts, graduation summary, open research topics count,
5 most-recent closes (with R_multiple), friction profile count + list.
iOS PaperTradingView (or a new AutoTraderCard view) can render the
full auto-trader status from this single endpoint.

5 new REST endpoints on /portfolio:
  GET  /auto-trader/friction                          - all profiles
  GET  /auto-trader/friction/{strategy}               - single (auto-default)
  POST /auto-trader/friction/{strategy}               - operator override
  POST /auto-trader/friction/{strategy}/calibrate     - on-demand re-calib
  GET  /auto-trader/dashboard                         - iOS rollup

(/auto-trader/status from Phase 1 unchanged — original
operator-state endpoint stays pristine for backward compatibility.)
