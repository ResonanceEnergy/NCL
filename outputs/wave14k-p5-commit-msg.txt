Wave 14K Phase 5: self-researching feedback (K4a + K4c + K4d)

Closes the meta-loop: SHAP attributions + bandit posteriors now feed
BACK into the upstream systems that produced the trade ideas in the
first place. The auto-trader stops being a one-way consumer of brief
output and becomes a learner whose findings reshape the next brief.

NEW: runtime/portfolio/auto_trader/self_research.py (~330 LOC)

K4a — apply_shap_to_authority_learner(attribution):
  Per-source lifts from SHAP top_positive/top_negative push synthetic
  outcomes into SourceAuthorityLearner. Lift > 0 -> "correct" outcome,
  lift < 0 -> "wrong". Count = round(|lift| * n), capped at n//2+1.
  Below-threshold lifts (|lift| < NCL_SHAP_AUTH_THRESHOLD, default 0.10)
  are skipped — too noisy to act on. Non-source features are ignored.
  Net effect: sources that PREDICT WINS get authority weight boosts in
  next morning brief; sources that PREDICT LOSSES get demoted.

K4c — generate_research_topics(lookback_days=14):
  Pulls recent closed losing trades from trade_idea_tracker, stitches
  in features from reasoning chains, clusters by (sector_etf, source,
  stop_type, rotation_quadrant) dimensions. Each cluster with >= 3
  losses (NCL_RESEARCH_MIN_CLUSTER) becomes a research topic:
    "Why did 4 XLE-sector trades lose (-0.77R avg)?"
  Idempotent: same cluster_features hash to same stable topic_id, so
  re-runs don't duplicate. FIFO eviction at NCL_RESEARCH_MAX_OPEN=12.
  Persists to data/portfolio/auto_trader/research_topics.json +
  research_topics_history.jsonl.

K4d — brief_context_packet():
  Multi-line text packet the brief executor prepends to its prompt.
  Three blocks:
    1. STRATEGY EXPECTANCY — top strategies ranked by bandit LCB
    2. STRATEGY LEARN — latest SHAP top_positive/top_negative per strategy
    3. OPEN RESEARCH TOPICS — losing-trade clusters awaiting research
  The brief uses this to bias trade-idea allocation (toward higher-LCB
  strategies, away from sources/setups matching open research topics).
