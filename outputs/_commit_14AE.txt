Backend:

  strips "submitted by /u/X to /r/Y" boilerplate, extracts subreddit from

  read-only over an existing file — no new ingest path. Wired into
  `_lane_intel(pack)` as `reddit_top10`.

  data is authoritative.

  REDDIT PULSE sub-block: chair narrative + numbered list of 10 entries,

  URL on its own line. `render_pro_brief` re-attaches `reddit_top10`
