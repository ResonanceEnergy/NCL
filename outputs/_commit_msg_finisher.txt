============================================================
J7c — Slippage tracker
============================================================
runtime/portfolio/slippage_tracker.py (~190 LOC)

SlippageTracker singleton; per-fill JSONL ledger.

Computes arrival_slippage_bps and vwap_slippage_bps per fill:
  arrival = (fill - quote_at_submit) / quote_at_submit * 10000  (long)
  arrival = (quote_at_submit - fill) / quote_at_submit * 10000  (short)
Positive bps = adverse (paid more / sold lower).

trade_idea_id field wires fills back to originating brief idea so
per-idea slippage feeds J1d expectancy.

by_strategy(lookback_days) rollup: arrival mean/median/p90,
vwap mean/median/p90, n_fills, total_notional_usd, plus _all bucket.

============================================================
J8d — Trade-date / settle-date split
============================================================
runtime/portfolio/settle_calendar.py (~170 LOC)

settle_date(asset_class, trade_date) honoring US holidays + weekend
slide:
  equity/etf/option/treasury/mutual_fund -> T+1
  future                                  -> T+0
  crypto/onchain                          -> T+0
  polymarket                              -> None (settle-on-resolution)

cash_view(trades, as_of) returns settled_today vs unsettled_inflow/
outflow vs trade_date_total. By asset class breakdown.

bp_view(summary, trades) computes trade-date BP (broker-reported,
includes unsettled) vs settled-only BP (subtracts unsettled inflow
you can't deploy yet).

US holiday calendar 2026-2027 included (20 dates).
