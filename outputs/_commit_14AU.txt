Wave 14AU: FinBERT + Twitter-RoBERTa wired into AWAREBOT scorer

Per FREE_RESOURCES_BY_LANE_2026-05-30 Tier D / cost-audit OSS
addendum recommendation: replace ad-hoc Sonnet sentiment scoring
in the AWAREBOT pipeline with local FinBERT + Twitter-RoBERTa
classification. Free, MPS-accelerated, ~5-20ms per signal vs
500-2000ms for an Anthropic round-trip.

NEW METHOD: Awarebot._enrich_signals_with_local_sentiment(signals)
  - Bucket signals by source: social platforms (reddit/twitter/
    bluesky/mastodon/telegram) -> Twitter-RoBERTa, everything else
    (news/RSS/YTC/polymarket/headlines) -> FinBERT.
  - Run both batches concurrently via asyncio.gather.
  - Stamp signal.metadata with sentiment_label, sentiment_polarity
    (-1.0 to +1.0), sentiment_model.
  - Best-effort: any import/load/inference failure leaves signals
    unmodified; the rest of the pipeline proceeds untouched.

INTEGRATION POINT: Awarebot._scan_loop
  Inserted after scan_cycle() and BEFORE score_signals() so the
  composite scorer can read sentiment from metadata.

SCORING IMPACT in Awarebot.score_signal:
  - Wave 14AU adds a sentiment boost to the actionability factor.
  - When |polarity| > 0.5, actionability gets +0.30 * (polarity - 0.5)
    capped at 1.0. So polarity=0.9 adds +0.12 to actionability;
    polarity=1.0 adds the full +0.15.
  - Rationale: strong directional sentiment implies the market
    reacts — actionability factor (10% of composite) is the right
    place to land the boost.
