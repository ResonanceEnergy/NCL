Wave 14J J1a+J1b: risk governor (heat caps + drawdown throttle, single gate)

Tier 1 Phase 2 piece #1. Composes J0b R-fields + J0c drawdown bucket
into ONE check_proposed_trade() entrypoint that every scanner / brief /
paper-trade calls before proposing new risk. The whole point of a single
gate: a new scanner cannot accidentally skip half of the check.

New module: runtime/portfolio/risk_governor.py (~340 LOC)
  Decision tree (first-match wins):
    1. band == halt           -> REJECT (mult=0)
    2. proposed effective R   -> if zero, REJECT
    3. total cap breached     -> REJECT
    4. strategy cap breached  -> REJECT
    5. else                   -> APPROVE or THROTTLE (if mult<1)

  Return shape:
    approved (bool), decision (approve|throttle|reject), reasons[],
    proposed_R_dollars, effective_R_dollars (after multiplier),
    strategy_tag, strategy_bucket, nav_cad, band, sizing_multiplier,
    heat {current_total_R, total_cap_R, by_strategy_current_R,
          by_strategy_cap_R, remaining_total_R, remaining_strategy_R}

  Default per-strategy budgets (% of NAV, env-overridable):
    total           10%  NCL_HEAT_TOTAL_PCT
    goat             3%  NCL_HEAT_GOAT_PCT
    bravo            2%  NCL_HEAT_BRAVO_PCT
    options          4%  NCL_HEAT_OPTIONS_PCT
    polymarket       1%  NCL_HEAT_POLYMARKET_PCT
    manual/unknown   3%  NCL_HEAT_{MANUAL,UNKNOWN}_PCT

  Strategy tag normalization (case-insensitive):
    goat | momentum               -> goat
    bravo | swing                 -> bravo
    options | theta | strangle |
      condor | put_credit | etc.  -> options
    polymarket | prediction       -> polymarket
    manual                        -> manual
    else                          -> unknown

  NAV floor NCL_HEAT_NAV_FLOOR_CAD (default 30000) ensures dev / pre-
  connect environments still produce usable decisions.

New REST endpoints (runtime/api/routers/portfolio.py +60 LOC):
  GET  /portfolio/risk-governor/heat   - current utilization snapshot
  POST /portfolio/risk-governor/check  - hypothetical trade approval

Scanner integration (runtime/api/routes.py +70 LOC):
  Both /stocks/scanner/goat and /stocks/scanner/bravo now compute a
  hypothetical R per result (5% of price as a placeholder stop until
  J1c lands operator-set stops) and attach:
    result.governor_decision = {decision, approved, effective_R_dollars,
                                proposed_R_dollars}
    scan._meta.risk_governor = {band, sizing_multiplier,
                                remaining_strategy_R}
  Soft-tag — does NOT filter results out. iOS + brief consume the tag
  to display whether a setup FITS in the current envelope.

Live-verified post-bounce:
  - smoke tests pass: normalize maps 7 aliases correctly; trade approval
    paths approve under cap, reject at total breach, reject at strategy
    breach, options bucket uses higher 4% cap
  - REST: POST /check goat $500 R at NAV $100K -> throttle (band=caution
    multiplier=0.75) -> effective_R $375
  - REST: POST /check bravo $4K R -> reject ("Strategy bravo heat would
    breach cap: current $350 + effective $3000 > strategy_cap $2000")
  - GET /heat: shows total_R $1350/$3000 = 45% util, goat 111% over its
    $900 cap, bravo 58% of $600 cap (legacy state from J0b tests)
