J2a — Greeks aggregation
  compute_position_greeks(): per-position delta/gamma/theta/vega.
    Uses broker-supplied Greeks when present, otherwise a BS-ish proxy:
      delta from cumulative-normal of moneyness (m = log(S/K)/(IV*sqrt(T)))
      gamma from normal pdf at m
      theta from extrinsic/DTE for shorts, BS proxy otherwise
      vega proportional to spot * pdf(m) * sqrt(T)
    Per-share values scaled by qty * 100 (equity multiplier); SHORT
    positions carry signed qty so theta correctly shows positive for
    premium-sell positions.
  aggregate_greeks(): net + by_underlying + budget envelope + flags.
    Budgets (env-overridable):
      delta:   +/- NCL_GREEKS_DELTA_PCT (default 30 = 0.30/$NAV)
      gamma:   +/- NCL_GREEKS_GAMMA_PCT (default 20)
      vega:    +/- NCL_GREEKS_VEGA_BUDGET dollars (default $200/IV-pt)
      theta:   NCL_GREEKS_THETA_MIN..MAX dollars/day (default 100..300)
    Flag strings surface budget breaches in plain English.

J2c — 21-DTE management trigger + pin-risk scanner
  dte_watchlist(threshold=21): SHORT options within N days of expiry.
    Recommends "close - gamma cliff" for <=7 DTE, "close or roll - 21-DTE
    management window" for 8..21 DTE. Gamma acceleration overwhelms theta
    benefit inside this band for short-premium structures.
  pin_risk_watchlist(pct=0.5): SHORT options expiring Friday within
    pct% of strike. Pin risk = spot close to strike makes assignment
    ambiguous; force-review so operator decides before close. Fixed a
    parser quirk where parse_option_symbol returns expiry as string in
    some paths and date in others — _coerce_expiry_date normalizes.

J2d — SPY->SPX substitution prompt
  brief_pipeline.py executor prompt rule 8a: every OPTIONS idea on
  SPY/QQQ/IWM at >=0.5R risk must include thesis annotation pointing
  out the SPX/NDX/RUT equivalent. Section 1256 60/40 tax treatment
  cuts effective rate from ~37% (ordinary) to ~27% (blended) on
  identical exposure. Skip for sub-0.5R sizes or stock-only trades.

New REST endpoints (runtime/api/routers/portfolio.py +95 LOC):
  GET /portfolio/options/greeks       - per-position + net Greeks +
                                        budgets + flags
  GET /portfolio/options/dte-watch    - 21-DTE candidates (?threshold=N)
  GET /portfolio/options/pin-risk     - Friday pin candidates (?pct=N)
