# CEO COMMAND DOCTRINE
## Chief Executive Officer - Supreme Command Authority

**NCC Integration Level:** PRIMARY STRATEGIC DIRECTOR  
**Authority Classification:** SUPREME EXECUTIVE COMMAND  
**Effective Date:** February 20, 2026  

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The CEO Command Doctrine establishes the supreme executive authority framework for the Super Agency, defining the Chief Executive Officer's role as the ultimate strategic director and command authority. This doctrine integrates CEO leadership with NCC autonomous operations while maintaining human oversight of mission-critical decisions.

---

## 1. CEO AUTHORITY STRUCTURE

### 1.1 Supreme Command Authority

The CEO holds ultimate authority over all Super Agency operations with the following command hierarchy:

```
CEO Command Authority
├── Direct Command Authority
│   ├── NCC Strategic Direction
│   ├── Council 52 Oversight
│   └── Executive Team Leadership
├── Emergency Override Authority
│   ├── EXECUTIVE_OVERRIDE Protocol
│   ├── Crisis Management Command
│   └── Mission-Critical Decisions
└── Strategic Reserve Authority
    ├── Board-Level Decisions
    ├── Major Resource Allocation
    └── Agency Mission Definition
```

### 1.2 CEO Decision Rights

#### Autonomous Decision Authority
- Mission and vision definition
- Executive team composition and compensation
- Major strategic partnerships and alliances
- Crisis management and emergency responses
- Board reporting and stakeholder management

#### Consultation-Required Decisions
- Budget allocations exceeding $1M
- Major technology architecture changes
- Significant operational restructuring
