# FRACTAL COUNCIL — Charter (v1.0)

## Purpose
Ensure FRACTAL FUTURE outputs are operational, testable where possible, ethically sound, and coherent across scale.

## Council Name
**FRACTAL COUNCIL (NCC)**

## Chair
AZ PRIME (NCC Governance Lead)

## Members & Roles
| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| **Evidence Lead** | Track 1/2 research quality; evidence grading E0–E4 |
| **Systems Lead** | AI/agent architecture; fractal intelligence stack |
| **Ethics & Harm Gatekeeper** | Prevent manipulation, coercion, unsafe claims |
| **Publishing Lead** | Outputs, narrative, share packs |

## Council Responsibilities
- Approve doctrine changes (v1.x → v1.y)
- Enforce gates (FF-1..FF-6) before publication
- Maintain taxonomy and definitions
- Track experiments and evidence quality
- Prevent manipulation vectors and harm
- Review entropy maps and budget compliance
- Monthly: produce "Approved Doctrine Delta" notes

## Meeting Cadence
- **Weekly:** 30-min gate review + evidence scan
- **Monthly:** Doctrine bump review + publish brief approval
- **Quarterly:** Full doctrine audit + track assessment

## Decision Model
- Claims require evidence labeling before council review
- Doctrine changes require gate pass (FF-1..FF-6) + majority agreement
- Ethics veto: any member can block on safety grounds (requires resolution)
