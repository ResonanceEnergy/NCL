## Map Template
All entropy maps follow the universal structure:
1. **Ingress** — Where disorder enters
2. **Accumulation** — Where it piles up
3. **Dissipation** — Where it exits safely
4. **Budgets** — Thresholds
5. **Valves** — Explicit mechanisms
6. **Failure Modes** — What fails first
7. **Blast Radius** — Local vs cascading
8. **Actions** — What to change next
