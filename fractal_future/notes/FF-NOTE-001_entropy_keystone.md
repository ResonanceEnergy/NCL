## Observation
During the initial FRACTAL FUTURE doctrine session, the concept of entropy emerged as the missing keystone that unifies the entire framework. The original 6 principles (P1–P6) described WHAT fractal systems do, but not WHY. Entropy provides the WHY.

## Key Insight
> Fractals explain structure. Entropy explains time, decay, pressure, and survival.
> Without entropy, fractal geometry is just pretty math.
> With entropy, it becomes a survival strategy.

## Doctrine Impact
- Entropy was elevated to Principle 0 (P0) — the root pressure that all other principles respond to
- Two new principles were added: P7 (Entropy Budgeting) and P8 (Entropy Valves)

- All existing principles were reframed as entropy responses:
  - P1 (Scale Coherence) = entropy dissipation across scales

  - P3 (Local Autonomy) = entropy containment through isolation
  - P4 (Graceful Failure) = entropy release without cascade
  - P5 (Resonance) = low-entropy coordination
  - P6 (Regeneration) = entropy reversal through renewal
