# Publishing Cell (FF)

## Mission
Convert validated findings into clean briefs and share packs.

## Responsibilities
- Compile weekly outputs into readable brief (1–2 pages)
- Create visuals and diagrams where helpful
- Maintain artifact index
- Ship monthly synthesis for NCC

## Outputs (Weekly Minimum)
- 1 publishing brief (1–2 pages)
- 1 visual or diagram (optional)
- Artifact index update

## Entropy Controls
- No publish without governance gate pass (FF-1..FF-6)
- Avoid "content for content's sake" — ship only what passes gates
- Retire outdated briefs quarterly
