# Entropy Controls Playbook

## Rule
> If entropy rises, reduce coupling and parallelism before adding complexity.

## Controls (Escalating)

### Level 1: THROTTLE MODE
- Reduce active workstreams by 50%
- Prioritize finishing > starting
- Trigger: any 2 entropy indicators at "high"

### Level 2: SYNTHESIS RESET
- Freeze new research until 1 L2 synthesis ships
- Clear the interpretation backlog before adding more raw data
- Trigger: contradictions > 5/week unresolved

### Level 3: PRUNE & MERGE
- Merge duplicates across all notes/artifacts
- Retire stale branches and outdated claims
- Trigger: backlog delta positive 2+ weeks

### Level 4: BOUNDARY REINFORCEMENT
- Rewrite artifact contracts with tighter scope
- Clarify definitions and success criteria
- Trigger: scope drift rate = high

### Level 5: GATE RE-RUN
- Apply FF-1..FF-6 to all active workstreams
- Reject outputs that fail gates
- Trigger: decision latency > 7 days or rework ratio > 0.5
