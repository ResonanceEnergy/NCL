# Super Agency Departmental Restructuring Report

## Executive Summary

The Super Agency has undergone a complete departmental restructuring to address disorganized agent roles and establish clear separation between operational departments and strategic oversight. This restructuring creates a hierarchical organization with defined authority levels, departmental responsibilities, and clear boundaries between council and operational functions.

## Organizational Structure Overview

### 1. Executive Council (AZ_FINAL Authority)
**Head**: Agent AZ - Council Chairman
**Purpose**: Strategic oversight and final authority
**Key Responsibilities**:
- Strategic direction setting
- Doctrine enforcement
- Crisis management
- Cross-departmental coordination
- Final authority decisions

**Separation Principle**: Council provides strategic oversight only, with no direct operational control.
